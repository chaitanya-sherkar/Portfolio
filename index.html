SEM Image Details Prediction

This project developed an automated method using CNNs and image processing to classify SEM images of metal powder particles into four shapes: spherical, angular, flakey, and irregular. It improves efficiency and accuracy over manual analysis, benefiting the additive manufacturing sector by ensuring consistent quality. Future enhancements may include broader categorization, real-time analysis, and adaptability to various materials and industries.

Thermoelectric Refrigerator

This project involves a compact thermoelectric refrigerator for pathology labs to store blood samples at 2°C–4°C using eco-friendly Peltier modules. It performs well under low and moderate loads but requires improvements for high-load efficiency, making it suitable for small-scale medical use.
